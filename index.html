Highlights of My Apprenticeship Training

  • Embarking on my journey as a Heat & Frost Insulator apprentice has been both a challenging and rewarding experience. With a background in front-end development, transitioning into the skilled trades presented new opportunities for growth, technical proficiency, and career stability.
  • Upon arriving in Canada, I explored various career paths that would provide stability and long-term professional development. My interest in the trades led me to the Heat & Frost Insulation industry, where I saw a promising career with strong demand and opportunities for advancement. After securing an apprenticeship position at Newcomer Centre, I began learning the fundamentals of insulation, including materials, techniques, and safety protocols.

  • Despite the challenges, I remained focused, consistently striving to exceed expectations. In the end, my hard work paid off—I successfully surpassed the program’s requirements and achieved exceptional results. This accomplishment not only reinforced my confidence but also solidified my passion for the insulation industry.
  • Graduating from this program has been an empowering experience, proving that perseverance and dedication can turn challenges into achievements. It has equipped me with the tools to thrive in the workforce and prepared me for the next steps in my career.
First Job at Nova Chemicals
  • After graduating from my training, I was fortunate to land my first job at Nova Chemicals in Blackfalds. This was a defining moment in my career, as it provided me with the opportunity to apply everything I had learned in a real industrial setting. The transition from training to actual fieldwork was both exciting and challenging.
  • At Nova Chemicals, I worked alongside seasoned professionals, tackling insulation projects that required precision, efficiency, and a strong understanding of materials and techniques. It was here that I truly put my skills to the test, demonstrating to my employers that I possessed the necessary knowledge and capabilities to excel in the trade.
  • This first job reinforced my confidence and affirmed that my decision to pursue insulation as a career was the right one. It provided a strong foundation for future projects and set the stage for my continued growth in the industry.
Future Goals and Aspirations
  • As I progress through my apprenticeship, my goal is to become a certified journeyman in the Heat & Frost Insulation industry by 2027. I aim to deepen my expertise in energy efficiency solutions and sustainable insulation practices. Furthermore, I am committed to continuous learning and professional growth, with the hope of mentoring future apprentices and contributing to the industry’s development.
  • I am truly grateful for the opportunity to be one of the students trained at the Newcomer Centre in Edmonton. The Centre played a crucial role in shaping my career in the trades, providing me with not only the technical knowledge but also the confidence and skills needed to excel in the Heat & Frost Insulation industry. The supportive environment and hands-on training I received allowed me to transition smoothly from theory to real-world applications. I will forever be thankful for the dedication and mentorship of the instructors, and for the Centre’s commitment to empowering newcomers like myself to succeed in a new country. Their investment in my future has been invaluable, and I look forward to continuing to build on the foundation they helped me establish.
